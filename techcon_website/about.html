History

TechCon began its journey in 2008 as a small, local tech meetup with a vision to bring together innovators and thought leaders in technology. The inaugural event, held in San Francisco, attracted a modest crowd of 200 attendees but set the stage for future growth with its engaging talks and interactive sessions.

Over the years, TechCon evolved into a premier global technology conference, now hosting thousands of attendees from around the world. The event expanded its reach, incorporating various technology sectors and emerging trends, making it a hub for industry advancements.

Mission

TechCon's mission is to inspire, educate, and connect the tech community through innovative discussions and networking opportunities. Our core values include:

Innovation

Celebrating cutting-edge technologies and breakthrough ideas.

Collaboration

Fostering connections between professionals and thought leaders.
